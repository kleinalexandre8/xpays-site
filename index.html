Compliance

Clear separation of roles

XPay is a business software provider. The platform is designed as a non-custodial orchestration layer and does not hold customer funds or customer crypto-assets.

Identity verification, KYC/AML controls, payment processing, fiat-to-crypto conversion, and settlement are performed by licensed third-party providers integrated into the merchant flow.
