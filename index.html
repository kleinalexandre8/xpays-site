Positioning

XPay operates as a software infrastructure provider for businesses. XPay does not hold customer funds, does not operate a consumer exchange, and does not perform regulated services directly.

Product overview

XPay is designed for businesses that need a more controlled merchant payment layer: checkout creation, payment state management, partner routing, webhook delivery, and reporting infrastructure.

Hosted Checkout

A clean merchant-facing checkout experience designed to guide users into partner-managed payment and conversion flows.

Merchant API

Payment creation, transaction lookup, status monitoring, and operational events through a simple integration surface.

Webhooks & Reporting

Signed notifications and transaction state visibility for internal ops teams and merchant back offices.

Compliance statement

XPay is a business software provider. The platform is designed as a non-custodial orchestration layer and does not hold customer funds or customer crypto-assets.

Identity verification, KYC/AML controls, payment processing, fiat-to-crypto conversion, and settlement are performed by licensed third-party providers integrated into the merchant flow.

Use case clarity

  • XPay is built for merchants, platforms, and marketplaces.
  • XPay is not a retail exchange, broker, or custody wallet service.
  • XPay provides software infrastructure and merchant operational tooling.
  • Regulated services are delivered through licensed integration partners.
